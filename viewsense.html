Trying to find innovation in image visualization

Back in 2013/2014 images on mobile phones were still being navigated as if we were looking at them in our computers. Their organization was based on the folder structure found back then in desktop operating systems and often the only way to find an image was by searching through countless folders.

Showcase of image galleries in mobile phones ca. 2013

Even though some companies like Instagram were offering different solutions to search images, there was no way of navigating images within a personal phone locally and besides this the solutions in that time were not mobile optimized.

The solution proposed in my thesis was based in the idea of creating dynamic collections by organising images across three dimensions, Place (where?), Subject (who or what?) and Mood (when or how)
