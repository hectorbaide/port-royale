ysura mobile

Adapting the power of ysura to your pocket

Project Type

Mobile adaptation of full featured pharma CRM

Project Duration

2 months part time

Role

Lead Design, research, concept

In a project needing a lot of collaboration with front end developers and product management we managed to shrink the core functionality of ysura CRM to the mobile phone. Daily actions such as activity reporting, access to detailed customer information and workflow approvals for events and contracting are now in the hands of hundreds of happy users.
