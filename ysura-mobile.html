ysura mobile

Adapting ysura CRM to mobile

Project Type

Mobile adaptation of full featured pharma CRM

Project Duration

2 months part time

Role

Lead Design, research, concept

After a highly collaborative process between Product, Development and Design, we managed to fit the core functionality of ysura CRM to mobile phones. By harnessing standardised tech and our preexisting backend framework, we were able to design and execute our first prototype in a matter of days and deploy a production ready version in a matter of weeks. The process in this case was slightly different than the normal design process in ysura, as we were iterating from low fidelity sketches directly to code, with quick prototypes being created and tested in a daily basis. After enough iterations and after validating our prototypes with customers we delivered an app which makes the life of sales reps and managers in pharma much more convenient.

Daily actions such as activity reporting, access to detailed customer information and workflow approvals for events and contracting are now in the hands of hundreds of happy users.
