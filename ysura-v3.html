work about

ysura version 3

From the fastest pharma CRM in the world into the most beloved one.

Project Type

Full redesign of a SaaS pharmaceutical marketing app

Project Duration

4 Months part time - 2 months full time

Role

Lead Design, research, concept, salesperson (to the company and to our customers)


ysura is a Munich based marketing technology company focused on the pharmaceutical industry. Over 1000 employees from the biggest pharmaceutical companies in Germany use ysura's main product ysura CRM.

ysura Version 3 was a project to redesign the front end of our product to prepare it for new the introduction of new, more powerful features and for cleaning up several parts which had become chaotic after a period of very fast growth.

Initial pitch for concept

While pitching internally we presented this idea to show how the app would become less cluttered and more understandable by the reduction of elements on screen and the consolidation of colors and controls.

This image was used in pitches to show that even though there would be changes, the impact wouldn't be so high that retraining would be required

Old vs new version
