From the fastest pharma CRM in the world into the most beloved one.

Project Type

Full redesign of a SaaS pharmaceutical marketing app

Project Duration

4 Months part time - 2 months full time

Role

Lead Design, research, concept, salesperson (to the company and to our customers)

What is ysura?

ysura is a Munich based marketing technology company focused on the pharmaceutical industry. Over 1000 employees from the biggest pharmaceutical companies in Germany use ysura's CRM.

Version 3 was a project to redesign the front end of our product to prepare it for new the introduction of new, more powerful features and for cleaning up several parts which had become chaotic after a period of very fast growth.

State of the interface before and after redesign

While pitching internally we presented this idea to show how the app would become less cluttered and more understandable by the reduction of elements on screen and the consolidation of colors and controls.

Old vs new version

A reduction in the colors used throughout the app allowed us to include graphs and visualizations without cluttering the interface or overwhelming the users.

Design component library

In order to keep consistency and accelerate our working speed we created a design library for use by the design and front end development team, a sample of which can be seen in the image below. All basic components plus some special use components were specified and the guide is used now across the company by PMs, designers and programmers.

Ysura design library

Results

After release, our customer satisfaction has gone up, our support calls have gone sharply down and our production times for new features have been considerably reduced due to increased consistency and better handling and company understanding of UI components and interaction principles.
